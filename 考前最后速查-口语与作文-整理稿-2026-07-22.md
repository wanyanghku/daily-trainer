# 口语 Speaking

## 二、考场总原则：把会的内容说清楚

### Part 1：10–20 秒，2–3 句

最稳结构：

1. 直接回答：`Yes, definitely.` / `Not really.` / `Only occasionally.`
2. 一个理由：`The main reason is that ...`
3. 一个小例子或对比：`For example, ...` / `Compared with the past, ...`

不要把 Part 1 说成 Part 2。答完两三句就停，让考官继续问。

### Part 2：先满足 cue points，再追求好听

一分钟准备只写四类词：

`who/where → when/how I knew it → what happened/details → why/feeling`

如果套母题，安全顺序是：

`题目专属开头 1–2 句 → cue 专属细节 2–3 句 → 已会母题核心 → 题目专属结尾 1 句`

不要让同一场考试里的 P1、P2、P3 全部回到 AI、PhD 或同一个细胞分割故事。科技题用 AI；日常题优先用香港、妻子、父亲、朋友、走路、羽毛球、Friends。

### Part 3：30–45 秒，3–4 句

最稳四步：

`观点 → because 理由 → for example 例子 → it depends / however 让步`

例子不需要统计数据，也不需要假装专家。生活观察、学校、香港交通、家庭和工作经历都可以。

## 三、P1 唯一需要补看的两个话题

### 1. Feeling bored

只记事实链：

`假期/项目空档 → 周一校长讲话和广播体操 → 跑步重复 → Friends S5E1 + 音乐 → 成年后更难兴奋`

**Do you often feel bored?**

> Only occasionally. I usually feel bored during a holiday or a gap between projects, when I do not have a clear task to work on.

**Did you ever find school boring when you were a child?**

> Yes, sometimes. I found the principal's speech and the school-wide morning exercises every Monday quite boring because they were repetitive.

**What sort of things do you find most boring now?**

> Running is probably the most boring thing for me now because it feels repetitive. I normally wear headphones, which makes it easier to continue.

**What do you do when you feel bored?**

> I often replay the first episode of season five of Friends, especially the London part with Chandler and Monica. I also listen to music because it helps me relax.

**Is childhood or adulthood more boring?**

> I'd say adulthood. As I get older, it takes more to keep me interested, so simple activities do not feel as exciting as before.

### 2. Daily routine

只记新增事实链：

`早上洗澡 → 上午做难任务 → 规律带来踏实感 → 晚上约 11 点睡`

**What is your daily study routine?**

> I usually take a shower and then go to the lab or the university library. My brain works better before noon, so I do difficult work in the morning and leave emails or meetings for the afternoon.

**Have you ever changed your routine?**

> Yes. Since coming to Hong Kong, I have made taking a shower every morning a fixed habit. It helps me feel fresh and ready to work.

**Is it important to have a daily routine?**

> Yes, definitely. A regular routine gives me a sense of calm and stability because I know what I need to do next.

**What part of the day do you like best?**

> Definitely the morning. My mind is clearer before noon, so it is the best time for reading difficult papers or coding.

**Do you often go to bed late?**

> Not usually. I normally go to bed at around eleven because I want to stay healthy, unless something truly urgent comes up.

## 四、P1 临场救场句：会用即可，不要每题都用

| 情况 | 简单说法 |
|---|---|
| 需要一秒思考 | `Let me think for a second.` |
| 从没想过 | `I've never really thought about it, but I'd say ...` |
| 没有相关经历 | `I haven't done it myself, but I think ...` |
| 不是绝对 | `It depends, but in my case ...` |
| 补具体例子 | `A simple example would be ...` |
| 对比过去 | `Compared with the past, I now ...` |
| 自然纠错 | `Sorry, what I mean is ...` |
| 不知道专有名词 | `I don't know the exact name, but it's basically ...` |

少用空填充词。`you know / actually / honestly` 偶尔自然出现可以，但连续堆叠不会提高分数。

### 3. 低频题调用短卡，不要硬拉长母题

- 环保法律 / 新法律：减少一次性包装；`optional cutlery + recyclable packaging + a small fee`。
- 动物故事：《西游记》；Monkey King / Pigsy / adventure / teamwork。
- 早起经历：重要任务或旅行当天早起；强调原因和感受。
- 禁用手机：医院、考试或正式会议；隐私、安全、尊重。
- 现场体育：一场篮球比赛；气氛、比分、和谁看。
- 电影 / 节目 / 有趣视频：Friends S5E1；熟悉、好笑、可以只听。
- 不享受的音乐活动：太拥挤、太吵、等待太久；不要说自己讨厌所有音乐。
- 名人 / 名人广告：明确人物或广告角色，避免只讲品牌却没有名人。
- 成功商人：好产品、理解用户、长期投入；不用讲复杂财务知识。

### 4. 一分钟准备纸的最小写法

例如“安静的地方”：

`Chi Wah / first week at HKU / several times a week / read + code / calm, view, long opening hours`

例如“家庭重要物品”：

`banner / patient's family / father helped them / kept for years / pride + responsibility`

只写名词和动词，不写完整句。准备时间用于检查 cue points，不用于默写母题。

## 六、Part 3 通用思考器

遇到任何抽象题，先判断它属于哪一种：

| 问法 | 思考顺序 | 可用开头 |
|---|---|---|
| Why / causes | 最直接原因 + 社会原因 | `One major reason is ... Another reason is ...` |
| Advantages / disadvantages | 最大好处 + 最大风险 | `The main benefit is ... The downside is ...` |
| Compare | 核心差异 + 原因 | `The biggest difference is that ... whereas ...` |
| Should / opinion | 立场 + 条件 | `I'd say yes, but only if ...` |
| Government or individuals | 谁影响更大 + 另一方仍能做什么 | `Individuals can ..., but the bigger responsibility lies with ...` |
| Changes over time | 过去怎样 + 现在为何不同 | `Compared with the past, people now ... mainly because ...` |
| Future | 大方向 + 障碍 | `I think it will become more common, although ...` |
| Different groups | 年轻/年长，城市/乡村，富裕/低收入 | `It depends on people's age, income and where they live.` |

如果没有例子，使用三种安全例子：

- `For example, in a crowded city like Hong Kong ...`
- `A simple example is what happens in schools/workplaces ...`
- `In my own family, younger and older people often ...`

## 七、Part 3 八个高价值话题池

每题只取一条正面、一条风险和一个例子；不要把整张卡说完。

### 1. 科技 / AI / App / 网络 / 新闻

**可用观点**

- 好处：save time, improve access to information, personalise learning, assist professionals。
- 风险：over-reliance, inaccurate information, privacy, less face-to-face contact。
- 最稳立场：科技是工具，关键在指导、核查和使用边界。

**可用句**

> AI can make learning more efficient because it gives immediate explanations. However, students still need to check the information and think for themselves.

> Online communication is convenient, whereas face-to-face communication is better for serious or emotional conversations.

### 2. 教育 / 学习 / 语言 / 孩子

**可用观点**

- practical skills + critical thinking should complement basic knowledge。
- group work gives different ideas; individual study gives deeper focus。
- rewards help short-term motivation, but interest matters long term。
- parents shape habits; schools provide equal access and professional teaching。

**可用句**

> Exams are useful because they provide a clear standard, but they should not be the only form of assessment.

> Children learn best when they understand why something matters, rather than simply memorising it.

### 3. 工作 / 成功 / 计划 / 商业 / 医疗职业

**可用观点**

- salary matters for security; job satisfaction matters for long-term motivation。
- successful people usually combine clear goals, adaptability and communication。
- plans reduce uncertainty, but overplanning makes people less flexible。
- healthcare work requires knowledge, patience and responsibility, not only high income。

**可用句**

> A good plan gives people direction, but they still need to adjust it when circumstances change.

> In the long run, people perform better when they find their work meaningful.

### 4. 家庭 / 帮助 / 榜样 / 代际差异

**可用观点**

- family gives emotional support and shapes early values。
- friends may give more practical advice because they share similar experiences。
- younger people adapt to technology faster; older people often have better judgment from experience。
- helping works best when it builds independence rather than doing everything for someone。

**可用句**

> Good advice does not always tell people what to do; sometimes it helps them see the problem more clearly.

> Older and younger people can learn from each other because they have different strengths.

### 5. 城市 / 建筑 / 住宅 / 商店 / 安静空间

**可用观点**

- cities offer jobs, education and services, but create high costs, noise and crowding。
- old buildings preserve identity; new buildings improve safety and efficiency。
- public libraries and parks provide low-cost places to rest, study and meet others。
- online shopping is efficient; physical shops allow people to see quality and support local business。

**可用句**

> Public spaces are especially important in dense cities because many people do not have much private space at home.

> Development is necessary, but cities should keep some older buildings that have cultural value.

### 6. 环境 / 自然 / 法律

**可用观点**

- individuals can reduce waste and travel cleanly; government and companies control infrastructure and large emissions。
- rewards encourage change; fines are useful when harmful behaviour must stop quickly。
- environmental education turns simple actions into habits。
- sensible rules need convenient alternatives, or people will resist them。

**可用句**

> Individuals can make better daily choices, but governments have greater power to change transport, energy and waste systems.

> A rule is more effective when people understand its purpose and have a practical alternative.

### 7. 交通 / 旅行 / 消费

**可用观点**

- private cars give freedom and comfort; public transport uses road space more efficiently。
- road trips allow flexible stops; trains and planes are faster and often less tiring。
- people overspend because of advertising, convenience, social pressure or buying a better long-term option。
- tourism creates jobs and cultural exchange, but can cause crowding and environmental damage。

**可用句**

> The best choice depends on distance and purpose: public transport suits daily commuting, while a car is useful for a flexible family trip.

> Tourism benefits local businesses, but popular places may need visitor limits and better waste management.

### 8. 娱乐 / 体育 / 音乐 / 电影 / 广告 / 食物

**可用观点**

- entertainment helps people recover from stress and creates shared experiences。
- live events feel more exciting; recorded content is cheaper and more convenient。
- sport improves health and teamwork, but competitive pressure can become unhealthy。
- advertising informs consumers but can encourage unnecessary buying; ads to children need tighter rules。
- traditional food keeps family memories and cultural identity alive。

**可用句**

> Live events have a stronger atmosphere, but most people use online content more often because it is affordable and easy to access.

> Food is not only about nutrition; it can also remind people of family and important occasions.

## 八、Part 3 卡住时的安全答案

**完全不了解政策题**

> I don't know the policy in detail, but in principle I think the government should make the safer or cleaner choice easier for ordinary people.

**问题太绝对**

> I don't think there is one answer for everyone. It depends on age, income and personal needs.

**需要推测未来**

> It is difficult to predict exactly, but I think it will become more common as the technology becomes cheaper and easier to use.

## 十、Task 1：五类图共用一个四段结构

### 通用四段

1. **Introduction**：改写题目，只说明图表展示什么。
2. **Overview**：写最显著的 2 个特征，不堆数字。
3. **Details 1**：把相似项目分成一组，给关键数字/阶段。
4. **Details 2**：写另一组，并做必要比较。

### 1. 折线图 Line graph

**看什么**：总体上升/下降、最高最低、交叉、峰值、稳定期。

**简单语料**

- `rose from X to Y` / `fell to Y`
- `increased steadily` / `declined slightly`
- `peaked at` / `reached a low of`
- `remained stable at around`
- `overtook` / `followed a similar pattern`

**Overview 模板**

> Overall, X increased over the period, while Y showed a downward trend. X also became the largest category by the end.

### 2. 柱状图 / 饼图 / 表格 Bar, pie and table

三类都用“大小、排序、差距、分组”，饼图不需要另背全文。

**简单语料**

- `accounted for X%` / `made up the largest share`
- `stood at X` / `was followed by`
- `was roughly twice the figure for`
- `the figures for A and B were similar`
- `respectively`

**Overview 模板**

> Overall, A represented the largest share, whereas D was the smallest. The two middle categories were relatively similar.

**表格提醒**：不要逐格抄。先找每列/每行的最大、最小和共同变化，再分两组写。

### 3. 流程图 Process

**看什么**：起点、终点、阶段数、是否循环、哪些步骤被动。

**连接与被动语料**

- `The process begins with ...`
- `At the first stage, ... is collected / placed / mixed.`
- `Next, ...` / `After that, ...`
- `Once this has been done, ...`
- `Before being ..., it is ...`
- `The final stage involves ...`
- `The process ends when ...`

**Overview 模板**

> Overall, it is a linear process consisting of X main stages, beginning with A and ending with B.

只客观描述步骤，不解释“为什么这样做”，除非图中明确提供原因。

### 4. 地图 Map

**看什么**：总体更住宅化/商业化/现代化；新增、拆除、替换、保留；方位。

**简单语料**

- `was built / added / removed`
- `was replaced by` / `was converted into`
- `was expanded` / `remained unchanged`
- `to the north of` / `in the centre of` / `along the river`

**Overview 模板**

> Overall, the area became more developed, with several new facilities and roads, while the river and the main residential area remained unchanged.

### Task 1 60 秒检查

- 有 overview 吗？
- 单位和年份写对了吗？
- 至少做了 2–3 个比较，而不是机械抄数字吗？
- 流程图是否用了正确的被动语态和顺序？
- 地图是否把 `was replaced by` 的方向写反？
- 有没有加入图中不存在的原因或个人意见？删掉。

## 十一、Task 2：五种题型只换“主体段任务”

### 1. Opinion：同意或不同意

`Introduction（立场） → 理由 1 → 理由 2 / 让步后反驳 → Conclusion`

立场必须清楚，不要结尾突然换方向。

### 2. Discussion + opinion：讨论双方并给观点

`Introduction（双方 + 我的立场） → View A → View B + my view → Conclusion`

两方都要真正解释；不要只在结尾用一句话给观点。

### 3. Problem–Solution / Cause–Solution

`Introduction → Problems/Causes → Solutions → Conclusion`

措施必须对应前一段的问题。例如“拥堵”对应公共交通、错峰、拥堵费；不要突然写教育宣传却不解释如何减少车流。

### 4. Advantages–Disadvantages / Outweigh

`Introduction（是否 outweigh） → 较弱一方 → 较强一方 → Conclusion`

如果题目问 `Do the advantages outweigh the disadvantages?`，必须明确哪边更重，而不是只列清单。

**安全立场句**

> Although this development has some drawbacks, I believe its benefits are greater overall because ...

### 5. Two-part questions

`Introduction → 回答问题 1 → 回答问题 2 → Conclusion`

先把题目中的两个问号圈出来。两个主体段各回答一个问题，是最不容易漏题的方式。

---

## 十二、Task 2 主体段的 6.5 安全链

> **One major benefit/problem/reason is that** ...  
> **This is because** ...  
> **For example,** ...  
> **As a result,** ...  
> **However, this is only effective if** ...

不需要每段五句都用连接词。真正的连贯来自因果顺序，不是 `Firstly / Secondly / Moreover / Furthermore` 的数量。

### 一个简单示范

> One major benefit of public parks is that they make exercise easier for city residents. This is because people can walk or run close to home without paying for a gym. For example, older people often use local parks for daily exercise. As a result, green spaces can improve both physical health and the quality of urban life.

## 十三、Task 2 补充话题语料池

已有范文重点覆盖科技、教育和城市交通。下面这些只作考前观点补充。

### 1. Health 健康

**观点**

- prevention is cheaper than treatment。
- parks, cycling paths and clear food labels make healthy choices easier。
- individuals control habits, but government shapes the environment and access。

**可用句**

> Governments can improve public health by making exercise and healthy food more accessible, while individuals remain responsible for their daily habits.

### 2. Media and advertising 媒体与广告

**观点**

- advertising informs consumers and supports business。
- it can encourage unnecessary spending and create unrealistic expectations。
- children need stronger protection because they may not recognise persuasive intent。

**可用句**

> Advertising aimed at children should be controlled because young viewers may not understand that its main purpose is to influence their choices.

### 3. Family, children and ageing 家庭、儿童与老龄化

**观点**

- parents shape habits and values; schools give professional teaching and equal access。
- childcare support allows parents to remain in work。
- older people contribute experience, but ageing societies increase healthcare and pension costs。

**可用句**

> Families have the strongest influence in early childhood, while schools become more important as children meet people from different backgrounds.

### 4. Crime, rules and punishment 犯罪、规则与惩罚

**观点**

- punishment protects society and discourages crime。
- prevention through education, employment and youth support addresses causes。
- serious violent offences need firm penalties; minor offences may benefit from rehabilitation。

**可用句**

> Punishment is necessary for public safety, but long-term crime reduction also requires education and employment opportunities.

### 5. Culture, tourism and globalisation 文化、旅游与全球化

**观点**

- tourism creates jobs and cultural exchange。
- overcrowding can damage local life and the environment。
- local traditions can adapt without being frozen; schools and festivals help transmission。

**可用句**

> Tourism can support local businesses, but popular destinations need sensible visitor management to protect residents and the environment.

### 6. Work and remote working 工作与远程办公

**观点**

- remote work saves commuting time and offers flexibility。
- face-to-face work supports collaboration and separates work from home life。
- hybrid work balances both when the job allows it。

**可用句**

> A hybrid system is often the most practical solution because it offers flexibility without removing face-to-face teamwork completely.

### 7. Government spending 政府支出

**观点**

- basic services such as healthcare, education and transport affect most people directly。
- arts, sports and science also create long-term social and economic value。
- the safest argument is priority, not “fund one and completely stop the other”。

**可用句**

> Essential public services should receive priority, but this does not mean that all funding for culture or scientific research should be removed.

### 8. Environment and energy 环境与能源

**观点**

- public transport, renewable energy and building standards create large-scale change。
- electric vehicles reduce local exhaust but still require clean electricity and charging infrastructure。
- rules work better when cleaner alternatives are affordable。

**可用句**

> Environmental restrictions are more likely to succeed when people are given affordable and convenient alternatives.

## 十四、Task 2 句型工具箱：够用即可

| 功能 | 简单句型 |
|---|---|
| 给原因 | `One reason for this is that ...` |
| 解释 | `This means that ...` / `In other words, ...` |
| 举例 | `For example, ...` / `A clear example is ...` |
| 写结果 | `As a result, ...` / `This can lead to ...` |
| 写好处 | `This allows people to ...` |
| 写坏处 | `This may make it harder for people to ...` |
| 让步 | `Although this is true, ...` |
| 加条件 | `This approach will only work if ...` |
| 比较 | `Compared with ..., ...` / `whereas ...` |
| 总结 | `Overall, a combination of A and B is likely to be the most effective.` |

## 十五、考前与考场最后检查

### 口语前 10 分钟

1. 看一遍 `Feeling bored`、`Daily routine` 两条事实链。
2. 看 P2 十篇母题路由表，尤其是 15 个容易套错的题。
3. 看 Part 3 的八个话题池，只选每类一个自己最顺的观点。
4. 不再读整篇母题，不再临时更换人物和故事。

### Writing 开始前

1. Task 2 题目划出：话题、限定范围、所有问题、是否必须给 opinion。
2. Task 1 先找两条 overview 特征，再写正文。
3. 先列每个主体段的一条中心句和一个例子，再开始写。

### Writing 交卷前 3–5 分钟

- 是否漏答题目的一部分？
- 每段第一句是否清楚？
- 立场是否前后一致？
- Task 1 overview 是否明显？
- `people is / government should to / informations / childrens` 等基础错误是否出现？
- 不确定的高级词能否换成更简单、确定的词？
